
    SQL LTD Code of Conduct

    At SQL LTD, our success is driven by a commitment to integrity, excellence, and mutual respect. Our Code of Conduct outlines the standards we expect from all employees:

    - Professionalism: Maintain a high level of professionalism in all interactions, both internally and externally.
    - Respect: Treat your colleagues, clients, and stakeholders with respect and consideration.
    - Confidentiality: Safeguard all company and client information. Sharing sensitive data is strictly prohibited unless explicitly authorized.
    - Compliance: Adhere to all applicable laws and SQL LTD policies.
    - Conflict of Interest: Avoid any situations where your personal interests may conflict with the interests of SQL LTD.

    Breaches of the Code of Conduct may result in disciplinary action. Please review the full policy in the employee handbook or consult your manager.
