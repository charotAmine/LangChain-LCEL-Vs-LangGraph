
    SQL LTD Employee Benefits Overview

    At SQL LTD, we value the well-being and professional growth of our employees. Here is an overview of the benefits you are entitled to as a full-time employee:

    - Health Insurance: SQL LTD offers comprehensive health coverage including dental and vision plans for employees and their families.
    - Retirement Plan: Employees can enroll in a company-sponsored retirement savings plan with a company match of up to 5% of your salary.
    - Professional Development: We encourage ongoing learning and provide annual stipends for professional courses, certifications, and industry events.
    - Work-Life Balance: SQL LTD offers flexible working hours and the option to work remotely up to 3 days per week.
    - Paid Time Off (PTO): Employees are eligible for 20 days of paid vacation per year, in addition to national holidays.

    For more details on how to access and take advantage of these benefits, please consult the employee handbook or contact HR.
